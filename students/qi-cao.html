Research Projects

Lead and support roles spanning national collaboration projects and major municipal initiatives on harmful algal bloom surveillance.

Haicha River HAB Monitoring (International S&T Cooperation)

Applied qPCR and ELISA workflows targeting mcyD and PC-IGS markers to quantify toxin-producing Microcystis during bloom events in the Haihe River, including cloning, sequencing, and strain validation.

YuQiao Reservoir Genetic Diversity Assessment

Performed multilocus sequence typing (MLST) across seven housekeeping genes to characterise Microcystis genetic diversity and constructed phylogenetic frameworks for reservoir management.

Haihe Microbial Community Profiling

Executed large-scale DNA extractions and 16S/18S rRNA sequencing for 200+ environmental samples to map aquatic microbial richness across Tianjin’s urban waterways.

Tianjin Hyperspectral Monitoring Platform

Installed and calibrated three ground-based hyperspectral units, developed eight water-quality regression models (R^2 >= 0.80), and contributed to a cloud-based real-time monitoring system.
