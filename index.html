Research

Huo's research centers on the formation of cyanobacterial diversity and the mechanisms by which it influences ecosystem function. By employing genomics, environmental microbiology, and bioinformatics, we investigate the environmental impacts of this diversity across various scales. Our findings are crucial for understanding the role of cyanobacteria in natural ecosystems. Additionally, our interests have expanded to include a wide range of ecologically significant microorganisms. Recently, we have initiated a genomics study specifically focused on filamentous cyanobacteria.
